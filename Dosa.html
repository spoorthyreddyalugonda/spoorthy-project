Masala Dosa

Dosa is one of the most popular South Indian dishes. It is a thin, crispy pancake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. Masala Dosa is served with spicy potato filling, coconut chutney, and sambar.

Why People Love Dosa?

  • Crispy and delicious taste
  • Healthy and light food
  • Served with chutney and sambar
  • Famous across South India
